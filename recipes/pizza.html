Pizza

Description

This classic delicacy will have your mouth watering before it is even out of the oven! Freshly made pizza with the right ingredients and seasoning is an absolute delight of a treat. The best part? You can make, with minimal effort, a first-class meal for you to enjoy using the following ingredients. Let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Capsicum
  • Corn
  • Olives
  • Pizza Base
  • Chilli Flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220Â° F
  2. Cut Tomato, Onion, Capsicum, and Olives and place evenly on base
  3. add cheese covering the surface
  4. Leave in oven for 10-12 minutes for the best texture
  5. Remove pizza from oven and add chilli flakes
  6. Enjoy!
