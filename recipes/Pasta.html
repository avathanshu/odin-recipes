Pasta

Description

What's better than a creamy, soft pasta dish with savories out of this world? With a delicious blend of sauces and flavor, enjoy a nice evening snack or experience a fine-dining italian cuisine through this recipe!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Corn
  • Olives
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Boil Water in a Pot
  2. Cut Tomato and Olives
  3. add Pasta to the pot
  4. Leave until water bubbles and pasta becomes soft
  5. Add sauce and mix, allowing it to cook
  6. Add vegetables and cheese
  7. Enjoy!
