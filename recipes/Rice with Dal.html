Pizza

Description

An Indian culinary delight. Sometimes, standard is the best!

Ingredients

  • Spices
  • Dal
  • Tomato
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Cut Tomato, Onion, Ginger, and Garlic
  2. Add oil to a cooker with mustard seeds
  3. Add chopped vegetables and stir while heating
  4. Add spices and salt
  5. Wash the dal and pour it with water into the cooker
  6. Wait for 5-6 whistles before turning off the stove
  7. Enjoy!
